Insights Analyst

LinkedIn - San Francisco, CA (San Francisco Bay Area)

Job Description

As a Insightss Analyst you will have the opportunity to work with one of the richest proprietary data sets to produce insights that support our field sales team, prospects and customers. You will work closely with our sales and account management team to provide data analysis support that will be used in producing powerful sales proposals, client business reviews and answers to specific client requests. In this role, you will become an expert at extracting data from our quickly expanding proprietary data set and turning it into business insights.

To be successful in this role you need to be highly analytical, have a passion for querying databases in SQL and be comfortable structuring ambiguous requests. You must be able to juggle multiple tasks in a fast paced environment and work with the sales team to figure out exactly what the client wants. Sales teams and clients won’t always know what’s possible, so it is important that you ask effective questions to get clarity. In addition you need to be able to work under tight deadlines in a fast paced environment and be able to effectively communicate across different functions.
